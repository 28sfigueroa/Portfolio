Twine

Reflection:

My twine story is about a tabby cat, Cabernet, who believes a robot(Roomba) has it out for her. She doesn't understand the robot (becasue she is a cat) and believes it is trying to ruin everything she does. She begins to believe it is breaking law one, A robot must not harm a human or allow a human to be harmed through inaction. Though the cat is a cat it is unclear of that throughout the entire story and it believes the rules apply to it as well as humans. Throughout the story Cabernet must figure out a way to overcome her fear of this robot and eventually learn to live with it! This is [[Cabernet vs. Robot]]
